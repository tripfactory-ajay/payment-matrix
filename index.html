🚨 Critical Thresholds - Board Approval Required

Financial Commitments Any payment or contract exceeding $5,000
DMC Agreements All Destination Management Company contracts
Acquisitions Any M&A activity, asset purchases, investments
Executive Compensation Salaries above $5,000/month or CXO level
Long-term Contracts Any commitment exceeding 6 months duration
New Offices Opening UK, Dubai, or other new locations
Banking Changes New accounts, signatory changes, mandates
Legal Matters Litigation, arbitration, settlements

📊 Approval Tiers Overview

Tier 1: Department Head Up to $2,000 • Email approval OK • Day-to-day operational decisions
Tier 2: Local Manager $2,001 - $5,000 • Written approval required • Dual signatory >$2,000
Tier 3: Board Above $5,000 • Board resolution required • Pre-approval mandatory
