Simplified Payment & Commitment Approval Matrix

TripFactory / ORN Vacations Pvt. Ltd.

Effective: DD/MM/YYYY | Version: 3.0-Simplified | Classification: Internal
1. Trip-Related Commitments

✅ AUTO-APPROVED

Any payment or commitment linked to a confirmed TripFactory booking reference is automatically approved.

  • Must include valid TF booking reference number
  • Must be standard operational expense (hotels, flights, transfers, activities)
  • Must be within standard supplier terms
  • Exception: New DMC agreements still require Board approval regardless of amount

Process for Trip-Related Payments:

1
Verify booking reference exists in system
2
Confirm supplier is approved (existing contract)
3
Process payment with booking reference quoted
4
Finance reviews monthly exception report

3. Always Requires Board Approval (Regardless of Amount)
Category Requirement Approver
DMC Agreements All new Destination Management Company contracts Board (Dual)

Self-Approval Prohibited: No individual may approve their own expenses, salary increases, or benefits. All such requests must be approved by the next higher level.
4. Emergency Override Procedure

In genuine emergencies where delay would cause material harm to the business:

1
Any Level 1 approver or above may authorize up to $5,000 verbally
2
Written justification must be submitted within 24 hours
3
Board ratification required within 48 hours
4
Finance maintains emergency override log
